How it works

1Create a project

Name the company you want to scan for legal exposure and opportunity.

2Gather the inputs

Browse Online pulls filings, judgments, gazettes and market signals from official sources for the period you set; add your own documents or firm briefs.

3Run the analysis

Dated legal signals become exposures mapped to practice areas, work products, buyers and time horizons, every claim cited.

4Get Insights

A filterable register, live charts, a trigger calendar and a shareable PDF report, with partner review built in.

Seeded with two live datasets: WhatsApp (Meta) and Reliance Industries · 31 public documents · 22 mapped exposures · every claim cited.
